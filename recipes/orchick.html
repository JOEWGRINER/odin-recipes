Orange Chicken

Delicious Orange Chicken

This Chinese Orange Chicken is made with boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces, dredged, and then fried until golden and crispy. The orange sauce is divine! It is a sweet orange sauce made with orange juice, vinegar, garlic, sugar, soy sauce, ginger, red chili flakes, and orange zest. It is both sweet and spicy and full of flavor.

This is the perfect chicken for the whole family to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Breast
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Orange Juice
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut boneless chicken to bite-size pieces and dredge in flour mix
  2. Mix sugar, vinegar, soy sauce together to make orange sauce
  3. Fry chicken in heated oil
  4. Toss fried chicken in orange sauce
