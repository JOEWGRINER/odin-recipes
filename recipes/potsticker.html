Delicious Potstickers

Potstickers are a type of dumpling. They are often filled with pork and cabbage, but can be customizable based on personal taste and preference. Potstickers are often seen folded into a pleated crescent shape, but can also be folded in half with no pleats for simplicity.

You can have these easy to make Potstickers on your table in no time

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Bok choy
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Place all filling ingredients into a bowl.
  2. Mix together until well combined. Set aside.
  3. Place wonton wrapper on a clean surface.
  4. Brush perimeter of a wonton wrapper with the beaten egg.
  5. Place a heaping tablespoon of filling in the center of the wonton wrapper.
  6. Open your dominant hand, palm side up, and hold fingers together. Place the filled and open wonton wrapper onto your fingers and fold the wrapper in half, only sealing the corner of one side.
