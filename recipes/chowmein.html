Delicious Chow Mein

This easy Chinese-inspired Chow Mein recipe includes noodles, vegetables and chicken with a simple chow mein sauce. It’s healthy and quick to make from home.

You can have this fresh chow mein on the table in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cabbage
  • Celery
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan or wok on high heat then pour in 1 tablespoon oil. Season chicken with salt and pepper and add to hot pan. Sauté until cooked through then remove to a plate. Whisk sauce ingredients together and set aside.
  2. Add another tablespoon of oil. Once hot, add cabbage, carrots, celery, and the whites of the chopped green onion. Stir fry over high heat for 1-2 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 30 seconds, then remove everything to a plate.
  3. Add last tablespoon of oil to the pan and add the noodles. Cook for 1 minute. Add the sauce and cook, tossing to coat then return veggies and chicken to the pan, along with fresh bean sprouts.
  4. Serve immediately, garnished with green onion.
